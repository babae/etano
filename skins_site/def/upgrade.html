If you are a customer who purchased Dating Site Builder from us and want to upgrade to Etano for free you need to answer a few questions for us. Please note that we might contact you for additional information if needed.

From the time you receive the temporary license number and download link for Etano from us you will be given 2 months to migrate your current site to the new site. After 2 months, your temporary license will be invalidated. Once you finish the migration process you need to contact us to receive your final license number.
After you finish the migration process, you must take down your DSB site and have only the Etano site up. If you want to keep your DSB site and have another one based on Etano, you must purchase one Etano license.
